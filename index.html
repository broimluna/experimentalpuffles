About

Experimental Puffles is a virtual world in development meant to be a "spiritual successor" to Club Penguin.
I am a small developer with an idea, and seeing the state of our online games nowadays, it's all about guns, or there's "weird" people.
Well not on here! This place is a safe place for everyone, including kids!
Talk, play, wander, Experimental Puffles is the latest place to explore!
